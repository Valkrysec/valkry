xOne App Intro

Welcome to the xOne App—your ultimate Web3 security companion. Designed by ethical hackers, this tool integrates with your browser to protect your decentralized journey. Connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask or Phantom) to unlock a dashboard of real-time stats—sites verified, security scores, and more.

Accessing Your User Dashboard

Get started with your xOne.app in a snap! Click "Launch App" above to go to your dashboard. There, connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask, Phantom, or any WalletConnect-supported wallet). Your wallet serves as your unique account identifier (name). You’ll see personalized stats like your Web3 Security Scores and Verified Transactions, reflecting activity from the xOne extension in your browser. For the best experience, use the same wallet here as with our Chrome app or extension.
