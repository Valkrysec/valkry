Security Features

xOne Secure employs a robust 5-step process to protect your Web3 interactions in real-time, ensuring your assets and data remain secure:

  • Step 1: Detect Web3 Interaction and Log Metadata – We capture every Web3 interaction, like wallet connections or transactions, logging details such as site URL, contract address (CA), wallet address, and timestamp to track your activity.
  • Step 2: Verify Site and Domain Safety – We check the site against phishing blacklists and reputation services to confirm it’s not a malicious domain, safeguarding you from scams.
  • Step 3: Verify Contract Address (CA) Authenticity – We validate the contract address by cross-referencing it with official token sources and verifying the deployer and ownership to prevent fake token scams.
  • Step 4: Analyze Smart Contract Code and Behavior – Our system examines the smart contract’s code for malicious patterns, like hidden fees or backdoors, ensuring it’s safe to use.
  • Step 5: Secure Interaction and Update Metrics – We allow or block the interaction based on our checks, secure the connection, and update your security metrics, viewable in the dApp’s “Web3” tab.
