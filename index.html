Security Features

xOne Secure’s 5-step process will protect your Web3 interactions in real-time:

  • Detect: Logs every Web3 interaction’s metadata.
  • Verify Site: Checks domains against phishing blacklists.
  • Verify Contract: Confirms contract authenticity.
  • Analyze Code: Scans for malicious patterns.
  • Secure: Approves or blocks interactions, updating your metrics.
