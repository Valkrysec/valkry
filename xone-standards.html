Our Commitment to NIST and NSA Standards

Watchdog Cybersecurity’s services are anchored in the robust frameworks of NIST and NSA, mandated for federal agencies and contractors handling sensitive data. We align with three cornerstone standards: **NIST SP 800-171 Revision 3**, **NIST CSF 2.0**, and **NSA Cybersecurity Guidelines**, ensuring compliance with DoD and NSA requirements while delivering best-in-class security for all clients.

NIST SP 800-171 Revision 3, finalized in May 2024, provides 97 controls to protect CUI in non-federal systems, critical for DoD contractors handling intellectual property or weapons system data. We implement controls like access management, incident response, and supply chain risk management to safeguard CUI. NIST’s machine-readable formats (JSON, Excel) streamline our compliance process, making audits transparent and efficient. Our team ensures your systems meet these standards, reducing the risk of breaches that could jeopardize DoD contracts. Learn more at NIST.gov.

NIST CSF 2.0, updated in February 2024, offers a flexible framework with six core functions: Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover. Mandated by Executive Order 13800, it’s the gold standard for cybersecurity risk management across industries. We use CSF 2.0 to map your assets, assess risks, and develop proactive strategies, ensuring your organization can detect threats early and recover swiftly. It’s like a cybersecurity GPS—our experts navigate the threat landscape with NIST as our guide.

NSA Cybersecurity Guidelines, driven by the NSA’s Cybersecurity Directorate and Center for Cybersecurity Standards, provide cutting-edge protections against nation-state actors. We align with **CNSS Policy 15-1** for cryptographic security, **NSA Cybersecurity Advisories** for real-time threat mitigation, and the **NSA/CSS Technical Cyber Threat Framework** to prioritize vulnerabilities. For example, we implement NSA’s **Protective Domain Name Systems (PDNS)** to block malicious domains, enhancing CUI protection for NSA and DoD contracts. Our collaboration with NSA’s signals intelligence ensures your systems counter advanced threats, as outlined in NSA’s mission to safeguard national security systems. Explore NSA’s standards.

By adhering to **DFARS clause 252.204-7012**, **CMMC requirements**, and **NSA’s cybersecurity best practices**, we go beyond compliance to deliver resilience. Our elite team doesn’t just check boxes; we anticipate threats like those from state-sponsored hackers, ensuring your systems are fortresses against adversaries. Whether bidding for DoD or NSA contracts or securing commercial networks, our NIST- and NSA-driven approach builds trust and delivers results.

How We Apply NIST and NSA Standards to Our Services

Our core services—Network Infrastructure Assessment, Auditing & Compliance, and Penetration Testing—are powered by NIST and NSA standards to protect and empower our clients. Here’s how we integrate these frameworks to deliver DoD- and NSA-grade security.

Network Infrastructure Assessment
NIST/NSA Alignment: We leverage NIST SP 800-171’s risk assessment controls (e.g., 03.11.01) and NSA’s **Attack Surface Management** guidelines to evaluate your network architecture, cloud systems, and endpoints. CSF 2.0’s Identify and Protect functions, combined with NSA’s PDNS, guide our vulnerability analysis.
Process: Our team conducts vulnerability scanning, traffic analysis, and configuration reviews to ensure CUI protection per NIST and NSA standards. We deliver a comprehensive report with prioritized remediation steps, formatted for DoD and NSA compliance.
Benefit: Your networks become compliant fortresses, resilient against advanced threats and ready for CMMC and NSA contract requirements.

Auditing & Compliance
NIST/NSA Alignment: Our audits align with NIST SP 800-171’s 97 controls, CSF 2.0’s Govern function, and NSA’s **Cybersecurity Advisories** to ensure compliance with CMMC, FISMA, and NSA standards. We use NIST’s machine-readable formats and NSA’s threat intelligence for transparent documentation.
Process: We perform gap analyses, develop remediation plans, and provide ongoing monitoring to maintain compliance. Our reports support DoD and NSA audits, ensuring no stop-work orders or penalties.
Benefit: Clients secure DoD and NSA contracts with confidence, knowing their systems meet federal standards and avoid costly non-compliance risks.

Penetration Testing
NIST/NSA Alignment: Our tests simulate real-world attacks per CSF 2.0’s Detect and Respond functions and NSA’s **Technical Cyber Threat Framework**, validating NIST 800-171 controls (e.g., 03.06.01) and NSA’s incident response guidelines. We align with CMMC Level 3 and NSA standards to counter Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs).
Process: Certified pentesters conduct tailored external and internal attacks, including social engineering, followed by detailed reports with NIST- and NSA-compliant mitigation strategies.
Benefit: Like cyber war games sanctioned by NIST and NSA, our tests uncover vulnerabilities, ensuring your systems are battle-ready for DoD, NSA, and commercial threats.
