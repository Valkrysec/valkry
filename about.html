About Watchdog Cybersecurity

Watchdog Security is a premier defense contractor specializing in cybersecurity solutions for Department of Defense (DoD) agencies and commercial businesses. As a small, independent team of 3–5 ethical hackers and engineers, we bring a passion for securing critical systems, offering tailored penetration testing, network assessments, and NIST-compliant auditing. Our focused expertise ensures compliance with standards like NIST SP 800-171 and CMMC, making us a trusted partner on gsa.gov and sam.gov for safeguarding government and enterprise infrastructure.

Our compact size allows us to work closely with clients, providing personalized, one-on-one service that larger firms can’t match. We are selective, only engaging with organizations that meet stringent NIST criteria, ensuring our efforts align with the highest cybersecurity standards. Driven by a mission to protect both government and commercial clients from evolving threats, xOne Secure combines technical prowess with a commitment to building resilient, secure systems for a safer digital future.
