Introduction

xOne Secure LLC is a premier cybersecurity defense contractor dedicated to safeguarding the critical infrastructure of government and commercial clients. Founded with a mission to deliver cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions, our small, elite team of penetration testers, systems engineers, and compliance specialists brings unparalleled expertise to every engagement. We specialize in network infrastructure assessments, auditing and compliance services, and advanced penetration testing, tailored to meet the stringent requirements of the Department of Defense (DoD) and enterprise organizations. As trusted partners listed on platforms like gsa.gov and sam.gov, we combine technical prowess with a deep understanding of federal standards to protect our clients from evolving cyber threats.

Our goal is to empower clients with robust, DoD-grade cybersecurity solutions that not only mitigate risks but also enhance operational resilience and regulatory compliance. We strive to deliver actionable insights through meticulous assessments, ensuring that vulnerabilities are identified and addressed before they can be exploited. Whether securing military systems or enterprise networks, xOne Secure prioritizes precision, confidentiality, and client-specific strategies to deliver measurable results.

At xOne Secure, we view cybersecurity as a partnership. Our services are designed to align with each client’s unique needs, from achieving NIST 800-171 or CMMC compliance to fortifying cloud environments against advanced persistent threats. By fostering trust and transparency, we aim to build long-term relationships that help our clients stay ahead of the cyber threat landscape. This documentation outlines our core service offerings, detailing how we protect and empower our clients to achieve their security objectives.

Network Infrastructure Assessment

Purpose: xOne Secure’s Network Infrastructure Assessment evaluates the security and resilience of your network architecture, cloud systems, and endpoints to protect against cyber threats. This service ensures your infrastructure is robust and capable of withstanding sophisticated attacks.

Process: Our expert team conducts an in-depth analysis of your network configurations, including routers, firewalls, and cloud environments. We perform vulnerability scanning, traffic analysis, and risk assessments to identify weaknesses. A comprehensive report is provided, detailing findings and prioritized recommendations for remediation.

Benefits: Clients receive actionable insights to strengthen their defenses, reduce attack surfaces, and enhance system reliability. This service is critical for DoD contractors and enterprises seeking to maintain secure, compliant networks.

Auditing & Compliance

Purpose: Our Auditing & Compliance service ensures adherence to federal standards such as NIST 800-171, CMMC, and FISMA, enabling DoD and commercial clients to meet regulatory requirements and secure government contracts.

Process: We conduct thorough audits of your systems and processes, performing gap analyses to identify non-compliance issues. Our team develops tailored remediation plans and provides ongoing monitoring to maintain compliance. Detailed documentation is delivered to support certification processes.

Benefits: Clients avoid penalties, streamline compliance efforts, and gain a competitive edge in securing DoD contracts. Our service ensures operational integrity and long-term regulatory adherence.

Penetration Testing

Purpose: Penetration Testing simulates real-world cyberattacks to uncover vulnerabilities in your systems, networks, and applications, helping you stay ahead of malicious actors.

Process: Our certified pentesters perform controlled external and internal attacks, including network penetration, application testing, and social engineering. Each test is tailored to your environment, followed by a detailed report with findings, exploit details, and mitigation strategies.

Benefits: This service identifies and mitigates critical risks, enhancing your security posture. It prepares government and commercial clients for advanced threats, ensuring systems are resilient and compliant with DoD standards.
