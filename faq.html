Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cybersecurity services for government and commercial clients.

What services does xOne Secure provide for government and commercial clients?

xOne Secure offers penetration testing, compliance auditing (NIST, CMMC), network security assessments, and remote support. We secure DoD and enterprise systems against advanced cyber threats, ensuring robust defenses and regulatory compliance.

How does xOne Secure ensure compliance with DoD standards?

Our team conducts thorough audits aligned with NIST 800-171 and CMMC frameworks, identifying vulnerabilities and implementing tailored solutions to meet DoD cybersecurity requirements for contractors.

What is penetration testing, and why is it important?

Penetration testing simulates real-world cyberattacks to uncover system vulnerabilities. It’s critical for DoD and commercial clients to proactively identify and fix weaknesses before malicious actors exploit them.

How does xOne Secure protect client data during assessments?

We use encrypted communication channels, secure data storage, and strict access controls. Our pentesters operate under NDAs, and all findings are delivered via secure platforms to protect sensitive information.

Why do your pentesters remain anonymous until contracts are signed?

Due to the sensitive nature of ethical hacking, our pentesters remain anonymous until contracts are finalized and a secure Teams call is scheduled, ensuring their safety and operational security.

Can xOne Secure assist with CMMC certification?

Yes, we provide comprehensive CMMC assessments, gap analyses, and remediation plans to help DoD contractors achieve certification and maintain compliance with cybersecurity standards.

How does remote support work with xOne Secure?

Our Remote Support portal, powered by TeamViewer, allows technicians to connect securely to client systems. Clients download TeamViewer, install it, and enter an admin-provided session ID to enable troubleshooting and maintenance.

What makes xOne Secure different from other cybersecurity firms?

Our small, elite team (3–5 experts) delivers personalized, high-impact solutions. With over 250 cybersecurity awards, we combine DoD-grade expertise with agile service for government and commercial clients.

How long does a typical cybersecurity assessment take?

Assessments vary by system complexity, typically taking 1–4 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the scoping phase, ensuring minimal disruption to your operations.

How can we get started with xOne Secure’s services?

Contact us via our assessment form or email to schedule a consultation. We’ll discuss your needs, scope the project, and provide a tailored proposal to secure your systems.
